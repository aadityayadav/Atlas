Connecting People
From everyday friends to club meetups in towns and cities, we create the opportunity to allow people to (literally) meet in the middle. Accommodating large groups of people has never been easier! Find a cute cafe, lush parks, or even a bowling alley. Make new friends, reconnect with old ones, Atlas makes it possible to create connections.
Building Communities
Ensuring communities get the same access to resources can be a challenge. Where should a town build its schools, hospitals, or even community centers? You could divide the community within sections and add weights based on the population size of each section. From localized centers, straight to the heart of a town or city, Atlas helps create a thriving community.
Efficient Resource Allocation
By seeing levels of energy consumption within localized communities, create weighted models that assigns greater weight to communities with higher leves of consumption. Use that information to localize energy plants to reduce costs of transportation.
